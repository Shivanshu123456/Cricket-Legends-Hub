Welcome to the ultimate cricket fan zone!

Cricket is a bat-and-ball game played between two teams of eleven players each. The game is played on a circular or oval-shaped field with a 22-yard pitch at the center. Each team takes turns to bat and bowl, with the aim of scoring the most runs.

Top Memorable Cricket Matches

2011 ICC World Cup Final: India vs Sri Lanka

A historic match where India won the World Cup after 28 years. Sri Lanka set a competitive target of 274, but MS Dhoni's iconic knock led India to victory.

2005 Ashes: England vs Australia (2nd Test)

Regarded as one of the greatest Test matches ever. England won by just two runs, leveling the Ashes series 1-1. A nail-biting finish at Edgbaston!

1983 ICC World Cup Final: India vs West Indies

A monumental match in Indian cricket history where India, led by Kapil Dev, defeated the mighty West Indies to lift their first-ever World Cup.

1999 ICC World Cup Semi-final: Australia vs South Africa

An unforgettable tie! Australia advanced to the final due to a superior run rate after a thrilling semi-final, known for the dramatic run-out of Allan Donald.

2007 ICC T20 World Cup Final: India vs Pakistan

The first-ever T20 World Cup Final ended with a dramatic finish as India defeated Pakistan in a thriller. Joginder Sharma held his nerve in the last over!
